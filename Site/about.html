About Me

My name is Joseph Frasier and I am a Developer. I have always loved tech and being able to create things out of nothing. My main goal is to be able to use my skills and then help people understand technology. Whether it's explaining a line of code or figuring out how to visualize data, I feel technology can be used tremendously to help people in their day to day life. I love everything Front-End. Creating websites, figuring which framework to use, how to organize it, you get the idea. I've handled a websites built on Wordpress, Squarespace and completely from scratch. Language-wise, I love me some Javascript, HTML, CSS, React and I'm familiar with Node.Js, MongoDB, Postgres.
